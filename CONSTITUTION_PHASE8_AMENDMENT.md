# SharipovAI Constitution — Phase 8 Amendment

Version: `2026.07-phase8-campaign-intelligence-v11`

Status: **Binding amendment to `CONSTITUTION.md`**

1. Phase 8 is an observation and analysis layer. It cannot create a second launch, order, credential or execution-authority path.
2. The one-second live view is read-only and sequence-aware. It may display canonical state, actual private fills, fees, divergence, drawdown, alerts and recommendations only.
3. Kill-switch, reconciliation, private-stream, heartbeat, campaign-blocker and drawdown failures must remain visible. Missing or degraded evidence cannot be rendered as green.
4. Post-campaign analysis is bound to one canonical campaign, its private execution evidence and its canonical final report. It stores an evidence SHA-256.
5. Execution-cost drawdown includes actual fees and adverse slippage. A higher canonical portfolio drawdown takes precedence when available.
6. A drawdown breach, missing divergence evidence, missing actual fees, fewer than 20 private fills or any identity/reconciliation failure blocks a positive recommendation.
7. Recommendations are limited to extended-Testnet promotion, hold-and-tune, reject-and-investigate or continue-bounded-campaign.
8. A recommendation is advisory evidence only. Automatic promotion is forbidden and a separate immutable manual decision remains mandatory.
9. Live monitoring and analysis cannot change credentials, runtime flags, the kill switch, capital, deployment state, experiment stage or Mainnet availability.
10. Phase 8 success cannot be claimed until 20+ authenticated private execution IDs, actual fees, clean reconciliation, the canonical final report and immutable analysis all exist.
