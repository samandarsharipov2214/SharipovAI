# SharipovAI Constitution — Phase 12 Amendment

Version: `2026.07-phase12-learning-validation-v17`  
Status: **Binding amendment to `CONSTITUTION.md`**

This amendment extends the complete Phase 11 Constitution. It does not replace, weaken
or override any existing execution, risk, campaign, CI, security or deployment rule.

## Evidence-driven self-learning

1. Outcome attribution accepts only verified Paper or authenticated Testnet evidence.
2. Synthetic, demo, mock, fixture and unverified outcomes are excluded.
3. Outcomes are immutable, idempotent and SHA-256 protected; conflicting identity reuse blocks.
4. Attributed PnL and drawdown must reconcile exactly to canonical settlement evidence.
5. Missing or non-finite numeric evidence fails closed.
6. Persistent agent metrics include sample count, direction accuracy, confidence
   calibration, attributed PnL/drawdown and regime/source coverage.
7. Challenger creation uses the canonical Experiment Registry with commit SHA, validated
   manifest hash, strategy/backtest configuration and immutable learning evidence.
8. Automatic research leadership is limited to **Paper research champion**.
9. Automatic execution promotion is forbidden.
10. Testnet, controlled scaling, deployment, capital, credentials and future Mainnet
    decisions remain separate evidence-gated manual decisions.
11. Challengers require sample, multi-regime, calibration, profitability, drawdown,
    walk-forward, benchmark and data-validation gates.
12. The self-learning supervisor is bounded and restart-safe. Its failure degrades learning
    only and cannot grant execution authority.

## Paper and Testnet validation

1. Expected-versus-actual Paper validation measures missing/unexpected fills, latency,
   price, fee and fill-ratio errors.
2. Paper-versus-Testnet shadow comparison measures latency, slippage, partial fills,
   fees, matched/unmatched identities and fill divergence.
3. At least 20 matched observations are required for promotion evidence.
4. Missing fills, duplicate identity, identity mismatch, non-finite evidence or threshold
   breach blocks eligibility.
5. Combined validation is immutable, SHA-256 protected and attached to the Experiment Registry.
6. Positive validation is evidence only. The final report, operator CLI and separate manual
   decision remain mandatory.

## Pre-merge, deployment and rollback

1. Merge requires the deterministic Phase 12 checklist and all required workflows green.
2. Preflight requires the exact reviewed SHA, clean canonical worktree, required target
   files and valid shell syntax, then delegates to the proven Phase 11 preflight.
3. Post-deploy verification binds host SHA, runtime SHA and OCI revision label to the
   reviewed commit and verifies database/self-learning readiness.
4. Rollback requires exact current SHA, reviewed ancestor target, backup, deployment lock,
   target verification and post-rollback provenance evidence.
5. Preflight, deployment, verification and rollback failures remain blocked evidence.
