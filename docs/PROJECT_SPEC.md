# SharipovAI OS Project Specification

## Mission

SharipovAI OS is an AI operating system designed to support market analysis, capital management, and investment decision-making. Its mission is to provide a structured intelligence layer that helps investors, analysts, and operators evaluate opportunities, manage risk, and act with greater clarity across global financial markets.

## Vision

The long-term vision for SharipovAI OS is to become a unified command center for intelligent capital allocation. The system will combine real-time market data, portfolio intelligence, automated research, risk monitoring, and specialized AI agents into one coordinated environment.

SharipovAI OS is intended to help users move from fragmented tools and manual analysis toward an integrated, adaptive, and explainable AI-driven investment workflow.

## Core Principles

- **Clarity over noise:** Convert large volumes of financial data into concise, actionable insights.
- **Risk-aware intelligence:** Prioritize capital preservation, drawdown control, and transparent risk assessment.
- **Explainable decisions:** Provide reasoning, assumptions, and supporting data behind every recommendation.
- **Modular architecture:** Build the system as independent components and agents that can evolve over time.
- **Human-in-the-loop control:** Support decision-making without removing final authority from the user.
- **Multi-market coverage:** Design for broad market analysis across asset classes, regions, and exchanges.
- **Security and privacy:** Protect financial data, user strategy, and portfolio information as first-class system concerns.
- **Continuous learning:** Improve models, workflows, and decision quality through feedback and performance review.

## Planned AI Agents

SharipovAI OS will be designed around a network of specialized AI agents, each responsible for a defined analytical or operational domain.

- **Market Analyst Agent:** Monitors price action, volume, trends, sector rotation, and technical market structure.
- **Fundamental Research Agent:** Reviews company financials, earnings reports, valuation metrics, competitive positioning, and business quality.
- **Macroeconomic Agent:** Tracks inflation, interest rates, central bank policy, employment data, GDP trends, and geopolitical risks.
- **Risk Manager Agent:** Assesses portfolio exposure, volatility, correlations, concentration, drawdown risk, and stress scenarios.
- **Portfolio Strategist Agent:** Recommends allocation models, rebalancing actions, diversification improvements, and capital deployment plans.
- **News Intelligence Agent:** Summarizes relevant market news, detects sentiment shifts, and flags high-impact events.
- **Crypto Market Agent:** Analyzes digital assets, token fundamentals, on-chain metrics, liquidity, exchange flows, and market sentiment.
- **Trade Review Agent:** Evaluates completed trades, identifies behavioral patterns, and produces performance feedback.
- **Opportunity Scout Agent:** Searches for emerging investment themes, undervalued assets, unusual market activity, and asymmetric opportunities.
- **Compliance and Policy Agent:** Helps monitor rule-based constraints, investment mandates, and internal capital management policies.

## Long Term Goals

- Build a unified AI operating system for investment research, portfolio management, and decision support.
- Integrate real-time and historical data across multiple asset classes and exchanges.
- Provide explainable investment recommendations with clear confidence levels and risk factors.
- Enable customizable agent workflows for different investor profiles and strategies.
- Support simulation, backtesting, scenario analysis, and portfolio stress testing.
- Develop a secure knowledge base for user-specific strategies, watchlists, research notes, and decision history.
- Introduce automated reporting for market summaries, portfolio reviews, and strategy performance.
- Create an extensible architecture for third-party data providers, brokers, exchanges, and analytics tools.
- Establish SharipovAI OS as a professional-grade AI infrastructure layer for intelligent capital management.

## Version 0.1 Alpha

Version 0.1 Alpha represents the first foundational release of SharipovAI OS. The goal of this phase is to define the core product direction, establish the initial architecture, and deliver the first usable components for AI-assisted market intelligence.

### Alpha Objectives

- Define the product specification and system roadmap.
- Establish the initial project structure and development standards.
- Create the first market analysis workflows.
- Implement early AI agent prototypes.
- Support basic asset tracking and watchlist functionality.
- Prepare integrations for market data sources and exchange APIs.
- Produce initial reports for market summaries, risk observations, and opportunity detection.

### Alpha Scope

The alpha version is expected to focus on research, analysis, and decision support rather than fully automated trading. Any recommendations generated by the system should be treated as informational output requiring human review.

### Alpha Success Criteria

- Users can define assets, markets, and watchlists for analysis.
- The system can generate structured market summaries.
- Early AI agents can produce explainable observations and recommendations.
- Risk and portfolio insights are presented in a clear, actionable format.
- The architecture is ready for future expansion into additional agents, data sources, and execution integrations.
